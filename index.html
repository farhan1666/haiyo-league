Tier 1

Magic the Gathering
Symbiosis
Financial Fraud

Passive Bonuses

  • Leagues XP multiplier is 5x.
  • Items from eligible sources are 2x common.
  • Farming ticks occur every 1 minute instead of every 5 minutes.
  • Minigame points are boosted by 4x.
  • Run energy is never drained whilst running.
  • All clue scrolls will drop as stackable scroll boxes, and clue step progress is saved between clues.
  • Players will only receive clue steps and port tasks they can access within an unlocked region.
  • Trees and mining rocks never deplete, and fishing spots are automatically tracked.

Tier 2

Conman
Holy Harvest
Perpetual Stew

Passive Bonuses

  • Leagues XP multiplier is increased from 5x to 8x.

Tier 3

Friend of the Forest
Harnessed Earth
Call of the Sea

Passive Bonuses

  • Combat experience (including Hitpoints and Prayer) will be multiplied by 1.5x. This is multiplicative with other experience modifiers.
  • The "Bigger and Badder" slayer unlock is unlocked for free.
  • Slayer reward points are 5x from tasks, and you aren't required to complete 5 tasks before earning points.
  • Superior slayer monsters will appear at a rate of 1/50.

Tier 4

Run to the Rescue
Industrial Evolution
Unleashed Teleport

Passive Bonuses

  • Items from eligible sources will be 5x as common.
  • Minigame Points received are boosted by 8x.
  • Leagues XP multiplier is increased from 8x to 12x.
  • Clue scrolls are now 2x more common from all sources.
  • Clue scrolls will always have the minimum amount of steps for their tier, and yield the maximum amount of rewards.

Tier 5

Lord of the Rings
Runic Freelancer
Faith

Passive Bonuses

  • A Black mask is now available at any Slayer master shop for 1,000 Slayer points.
  • You may now purchase a specific Slayer task, including amount to kill, for 100 Slayer points each.

Tier 6

Feral Exorcism
Papercrafting
Mariner

Passive Bonuses

  • Leagues XP multiplier is increased from 12x to 16x.

Tier 7

Reaper's Avarice
Raining Luck
Squeal of Fortune

Passive Bonuses

  • Reloaded: In addition to choosing a relic from this tier, you can unlock any additional relic from Tier 6 and below.

Tier 8

Grandmaster at Arms
Dragon Multicannon
Leap

Passive Bonuses

  • Preserve, Chivalry, Piety, Rigour and Augury are automatically unlocked, provided you have the Prayer level to use them.
  • With Level 99 Herblore, you can now mix a Super combat potion, a Ranging potion, a Magic potion, and an Onion together to create an Overload (Nightmare Zone version).

Welcome

To best understand these relics, you'll need some essential context:

  • All players start in Misthalin.
  • Sailing and all its related content would be present in this League.
    • You would only have access to the oceans which you can directly sail into from docking points in areas you've unlocked (e.g. Misthalin grants access to the Ardent Ocean. Varlamore grants access to the Western and Sunset Oceans).
  • A separate system for combat, more similar to Combat Masteries than Demonic Pacts, would coexist with these relics. This is why there are only a few combat oriented choices here.
    • If you'd be interested in seeing these, let us know!
  • Most passive bonuses are the same as in Leagues 6: Demonic Pacts. New passive bonuses are listed in orange.
